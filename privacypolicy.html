Privacy Policy - Arrive Alert

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

Location Data

Arrive Alert collects your location data solely to provide location-based alarm functionality. This data:

  • Is used only to monitor your approach to alarm destinations
  • Is processed locally on your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties

How We Use Your Information

Your location data is used exclusively to:

  • Set up geofenced alarm regions
  • Trigger notifications when you approach destinations
  • Store alarm history locally on your device

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Apple Maps: Used for map display and geocoding services.

Google AdMob: Displays advertisements in the free version.

Apple App Store: Processes premium subscription payments.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable location access at any time in iOS Settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

Contact Us

For privacy questions: support@arrivealert.app

This policy complies with GDPR and CCPA requirements.
