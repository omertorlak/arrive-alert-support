❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set a location alarm?

A: Long press anywhere on the map to place a pin, adjust the radius, and tap "Set Alarm".

Q: Why isn't my alarm triggering?

A: Ensure location permission is set to "Always" and Background App Refresh is enabled.

Q: How do I cancel a subscription?

A: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Arrive Alert → Cancel.

Q: The app isn't working in background

A: Check Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Arrive Alert → "Always"
